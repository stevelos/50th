Steve's Party Menu

Delight in the unique culinary journey of bowl food, a party food which is as convient as canapes but distinctly more filling and yet around a quarter the size of a formal meal. Each dish is a symphony of flavors and textures, perfectly contained in a single, elegant bowl.

This exclusive menu will be served from 8 to 9 pm. During this time, attentive service staff will circulate the room, allowing you the convenience of selecting your preferred dishes directly from them. This personalized approach ensures a seamless and delightful dining experience.
